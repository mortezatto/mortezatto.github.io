Biography

I recently (Feb 2024) defended my Master's thesis in Computer Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence at the esteemed Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS) under supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Mahdi Bohlouli.
During my studies, I had the opportunity to gain valuable teaching experience as a lecturer assistant for a course.
In my master's thesis, I conducted an in-depth study of ECG signals using the MIT-BIH datasets. This rigorous work, which involved advanced methodologies such as Conditional Generative Adversarial Networks (C-GAN) and Transfer Learning, was recognized for its excellence during a highly successful defense that I completed with an outstanding evaluation.
The primary objective of my work is to develop a robust monitoring system capable of providing early warnings for potential health problems, thereby significantly enhancing patient care and well-being.
